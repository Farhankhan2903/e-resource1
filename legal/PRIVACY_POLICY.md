# Privacy Policy & User Data Collection

**Last Updated: May 2024**

E-Resource Solution ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

## 1. Data We Collect
*   **Personal Information**: Name, email address, mobile number, and profile photo.
*   **Identity Documents (Workers Only)**: Masked Aadhaar number, PAN number, and Selfie for KYC verification.
*   **Location Data**: Real-time GPS coordinates for nearby service discovery.
*   **Transaction Data**: Booking history, tool rentals, and payment invoices.

## 2. Purpose of Collection
*   To verify the identity of technicians (KYC).
*   To facilitate connections between customers and service providers based on proximity.
*   To generate accurate tax invoices and financial reports.
*   To improve the AI diagnosis engine based on anonymized fault reports.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
*   All sensitive documents (Aadhaar/PAN) are encrypted and stored in secure data centers.
*   We use Industry-standard JWT tokens for session management and API security.
*   Passwords are never stored in plain text; we use **bcrypt** hashing.

## 4. Data Sharing
*   We do not sell your data to third parties.
*   Limited information (Name, Phone) is shared with the other party (Customer/Worker) only when a booking is accepted.

## 5. Your Rights
*   You may request to delete your account and associated data at any time through the Profile settings.
*   You can opt-out of location tracking by disabling GPS permissions in the App settings.

## 6. Compliance
This policy is designed to comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act of India and relevant global data protection principles.
